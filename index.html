How it works

One booking, five honest states.

Every job in CleanApp Go moves through the same pipeline our backend enforces — nothing is marked "done" or "paid" until it actually is.

PENDING

You request a booking

Pick a cleaner, a service offering, and a time. The price and commission split are locked in immediately.

ACCEPTED

Your cleaner confirms

The cleaner accepts the job. You can chat, reschedule, or cancel from the booking screen.

IN PROGRESS

The clean happens

Your cleaner starts the job on-site at the scheduled time.

COMPLETED

Work is marked done

Only now does payment open — you're never charged for a clean that hasn't happened.

PAID

You pay & rate

Settle securely via Paystack or ExpressPay, then rate your cleaner. Funds release to them automatically.

Questions

Good to know before you book.

When am I actually charged?

Only after your cleaner marks the job "completed." You can review the itemized total in the app before paying via Paystack or ExpressPay.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can cancel or request a new date directly from the booking screen. See our Refund & Cancellation Policy for the specifics on timing and refunds.

Are cleaners background-checked?

Cleaner profiles and the specific services they list are reviewed and approved by our team before they appear to customers. Approval can be revoked for policy violations.

Which areas do you cover?

We're onboarding cleaners and customers across Accra, Ghana, starting with the highest-demand neighbourhoods first.

How do I become a cleaner on the platform?

Reach out via the contact section below — we'll walk you through profile approval and listing your services.
